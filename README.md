# Utsav Prabhu

I am a Senior Staff Research Engineer at Google DeepMind, where I focus on AI safety, alignment, and the responsible development of frontier models. My work involves building autonomous agents for AI development and ensuring fairness and robustness in the deployment of AI technologies across Google products.

* **Human-Centric Perception:** Developing efficient computer vision technologies for people analytics and assistant devices.
* **Automating AI Development:** Engineering agents for co-improvement and autonomous development pipelines.
